FUNDAMENTALS

THE IMPORTANCE OF ONE OF THE DANCERS’ FUNDAMENTAL STEP Even if it is sometimes considered, by dancers, a “boring” exercise, Plies are one of the fundamental movements in ballet. The term Plies comes from the French language and means “bent”. It is an action that is made by the knees, followed by the hip joint and ankle.
